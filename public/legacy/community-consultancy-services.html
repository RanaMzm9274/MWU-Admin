Professional Consultancy

Research-backed advisory services for institutions, businesses, and development partners

Madda Walabu University provides consultancy through academic and technical experts who combine research capacity with practical field experience. Services support planning, institutional improvement, technical problem solving, and implementation follow-through.

Business Development

  • Market research and feasibility studies.
  • Business plan and strategic planning support.
  • Performance improvement and growth planning.

Organisational Development

  • Organisational design and restructuring.
  • Training needs assessment.
  • Change management and capacity building.

Technical Consultancy

  • Engineering and infrastructure advisory.
  • Modern agriculture and precision farming support.
  • Digital transformation, IT solutions, and health system strengthening.
