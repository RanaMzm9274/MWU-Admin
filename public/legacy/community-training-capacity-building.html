Skills for Sustainable Development

Training programmes for individuals, institutions, and emerging entrepreneurs

MWU designs training and capacity building initiatives that strengthen practical skills, leadership, technical competence, and entrepreneurship. Programmes are delivered through expert trainers, practical sessions, and post-training mentorship.

Professional Development

  • Leadership and management training.
  • Project management certification support.
  • Communication and soft skills development.
  • Workplace performance improvement.

Technical Skills Training

  • Digital literacy and computer skills.
  • Agricultural technology and modern farming.
  • Vocational skills and craftsmanship.
  • Practical workforce readiness training.

Entrepreneurship and Business

  • Business planning and financial management.
  • Marketing and digital business strategy.
  • Startup incubation and mentoring.
  • Small business growth support.
Training Process

How programmes are developed and delivered

1

Needs Assessment

Identify skill gaps, participant needs, and institutional goals.

2

Programme Design

Create customised curriculum, materials, and delivery plans.

3

Training Delivery

Run interactive sessions, demonstrations, and applied exercises.

4

Evaluation

Assess outcomes and provide ongoing mentoring where needed.
