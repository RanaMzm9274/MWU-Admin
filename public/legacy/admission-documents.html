Application Checklist

Prepare Your Admission File

Core Documents

  • Completed application form.
  • Educational certificates.
  • Academic transcripts.
  • National examination results.
  • Identification documents.

Department Documents

  • Additional materials required by departments.
  • Entrance examination or interview materials where required.
  • Research interests or proposal for PhD programs where required.
  • Evidence for advanced standing or transferable credits.

Verification

The Registrar Office verifies authenticity, academic qualifications, entrance eligibility, and Ministry of Education compliance. Academic Units evaluate credentials from other institutions.
