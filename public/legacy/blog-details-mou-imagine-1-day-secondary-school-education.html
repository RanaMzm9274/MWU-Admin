Madda Walabu University has signed MoU with Imagine 1 Day International Organization to Strengthen Secondary School Education

  • Home
  • Blog

by MWU Public and International Relations Office February 2026 Partnership

Madda Walabu University has officially signed a Memorandum of Understanding with Imagine 1 Day International Organization in a significant step toward improving the quality of secondary school education through collaborative educational development programs.

The agreement is designed to strengthen secondary school education by empowering teachers, school leaders, and students through capacity building training, educational support, leadership development, and community-centered learning initiatives.

Key Information

  • Partnership focus: Strengthening secondary school education
  • Core areas: Capacity building, educational support, leadership development, and community-centered learning
  • Coverage: Oromia and beyond
  • Shared goal: Sustainable improvement in teaching quality and student achievement

Signing Ceremony Highlights

  • Dr. Berhanemeskel Tena, President of Madda Walabu University, spoke on the importance of educational transformation through partnership
  • The President emphasized that the MoU creates an opportunity for lasting educational change
  • Mr. Gosaye Gizaw, Oromia Teaching Program Coordinator for Imagine 1 Day International Organization, outlined the organization’s education support focus
  • Both institutions reaffirmed their shared commitment to quality education and institutional collaboration

Strengthening secondary education is essential for producing competent and responsible citizens who can contribute positively to national development.

Dr. Berhanemeskel Tena

Procedure and Partnership Scope

  • Implement capacity building training for teachers and school leaders
  • Support student learning through educational initiatives
  • Promote research activities and school improvement projects
  • Strengthen institutional capacity through joint programming

Important Notes

The partnership is expected to contribute to sustainable improvements in teaching quality and student achievement. It also reflects MWU’s readiness to work with national and international organizations to improve access to quality education, enhance teaching methodologies, and support school leadership systems.

Representatives from both institutions agreed to work together in addressing educational challenges and creating more effective and inclusive learning environments for teachers and students.

Official Confirmation

According to the Public and International Relations Office of Madda Walabu University, the MoU represents a strong institutional commitment to promoting quality education, professional development, and better opportunities for learners and educators.

Source: Madda Walabu University Public & International Relations Office.
