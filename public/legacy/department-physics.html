Program Learning Objectives

  • Build strong theoretical, experimental and computational physics foundations.
  • Develop laboratory, data-analysis, scientific communication and research competence.
  • Apply physics to real-world scientific and technological challenges.
  • Promote ethics, leadership, teamwork, independent learning and lifelong professional development.
