Bidire Research Center

The Bidire Research Center focuses on agricultural innovation, sustainable development, and community empowerment. Located in the heart of Ethiopia's agricultural region, the center addresses critical challenges in food security, rural development, and environmental sustainability.

  • Agricultural Excellence and food security research
  • Improved farming practices through community partnerships
  • Applied research for rural development and sustainability
