Ginhir Research Center

The Ginhir Research Center specializes in environmental conservation, biodiversity research, and sustainable natural resource management. The center works at the intersection of scientific research and community engagement to protect and restore local ecosystems.

  • Environmental Stewardship and biodiversity research
  • Sustainable natural resource management
  • Community-based conservation and environmental awareness
