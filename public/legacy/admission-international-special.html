International Student Admission

  • Meet equivalent academic standards.
  • Submit valid passport or identification documents.
  • Submit residence or refugee documentation where applicable.
  • Fulfill university and national regulations for international admission.

Special Admission Consideration

MWU may provide affirmative consideration for female applicants, students with disabilities, educationally disadvantaged groups, and other equity cases approved by Academic Units and the Admissions and Placement Committee.

No special admission criteria may be implemented without prior APC approval.
