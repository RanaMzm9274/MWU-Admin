Campuses Built Around Access, Service, and Regional Impact

Madda Walabu University is based in Bale Robe, Oromia, with additional academic and health-science activity in Shashemene and Goba. The campuses support teaching, research, community service, healthcare training, and student life across southeast Ethiopia.

Main Campus

The Robe Main Campus is the university's principal academic and administrative home, located in Bale Robe, about 430 km from Addis Ababa.

  • Bale Robe, Oromia Regional State
  • Central administration and academic colleges
  • Research, innovation, and student support services

Shashemene Campus

Shashemene Campus extends MWU's regional reach in West Arsi, supporting campus-based learning and continuing education opportunities.

  • Shashemene City, West Arsi
  • Regional access point for students and communities
  • Undergraduate and continuing education hub

Goba Referral Hospital

MWU Goba Teaching Hospital supports medical education, health research, and healthcare service delivery for Bale, East Bale, West Arsi, Guji, and part of Somali Region.

  • Goba, Ethiopia
  • Clinical training for health science programs
  • Teaching, research, and patient care services
