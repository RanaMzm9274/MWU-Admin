About the ceremony

Madda Walabu University proudly celebrated its 18th Graduation Ceremony on June 27, 2026, awarding Bachelor's and Master's degrees to 1,089 graduates from its various colleges. The ceremony brought together graduates, families, academic staff, university leadership, and invited guests to recognize years of study, sacrifice, mentorship, and achievement.

The graduation ceremony marked both an academic milestone and a public celebration of the University's continued contribution to national development. Through its colleges and programs, MWU continues to prepare graduates who can serve communities, strengthen institutions, support innovation, and contribute to Ethiopia's social and economic progress.

Ceremony highlights

The 18th Graduation Ceremony honored the achievement of students completing rigorous academic training and entering a new phase of professional responsibility. The event also recognized the role of families, instructors, advisors, and administrative staff who supported the graduates throughout their academic journey.

President's Message

A New Journey of Responsibility and Service

In his congratulatory address, University President Dr. Berhanemeskel Tena congratulated the graduates and their families, describing graduation as the beginning of a new journey of responsibility, service, and opportunity. He encouraged graduates to uphold professionalism, integrity, and compassion throughout their careers and reminded them that education carries a duty to serve communities with humility and purpose.

Dr. Berhanemeskel also reflected on the rapidly changing digital world. He noted that Artificial Intelligence and digital technologies are transforming education, workplaces, public service, and social life, but emphasized that technology can never replace human empathy, ethical judgment, and dedication to serving society. His message called on graduates to use knowledge and technology responsibly while keeping people, communities, and moral values at the center of their work.

The ceremony concluded with the presentation of medals and trophies to outstanding graduates in recognition of academic excellence. These recognitions celebrated high performance, perseverance, and the commitment to learning that the University seeks to encourage across all graduating classes.

As the graduates begin the next chapter of their lives, Madda Walabu University expressed confidence that they will serve as ambassadors of the institution, contribute to national development, and carry forward the University's values of excellence, responsibility, and public service.
