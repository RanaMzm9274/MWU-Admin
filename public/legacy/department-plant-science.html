About the Department

Established in 2007, the department produces competent graduates, conducts impactful research and delivers community services supporting sustainable agriculture and food security. Its focus includes crop production, plant breeding, genetics, plant protection, biotechnology, agronomy and climate-smart agriculture.

Program Learning Objectives

  • Improve food security through crop productivity and resilience.
  • Promote climate-smart agriculture and environmental sustainability.
  • Apply plant breeding, crop production, pest management and biotechnology.
  • Integrate digital agriculture and precision farming.
  • Build research, extension, entrepreneurship, leadership and ethical practice.
