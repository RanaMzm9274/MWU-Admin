Scholarship Criteria

Scholarship Criteria of Madda Walabu University

Administration

The University Senate Legislation establishes scholarship administration under the Academic Staff Affairs Committee (ASAC). The ASAC is responsible for staff development policies, scholarship administration, selection of candidates for training and research opportunities, and approval of further study opportunities.

Eligibility

  • Strong academic performance.
  • Satisfactory CGPA.
  • Professional competence.
  • Good disciplinary standing.
  • Institutional relevance and manpower need.

Categories

  • Postgraduate sponsorship.
  • Research scholarships.
  • Staff development scholarships.
  • Sabbatical and study leave support.
  • Training opportunities and external fellowship programs.

Selection Principles

Scholarship and training opportunities are competitive. The ASAC reviews scholarship applications and ensures fairness, transparency, gender balance, social inclusion, and equal opportunity principles.

Continuation Conditions

Beneficiaries are expected to maintain satisfactory academic performance, submit progress reports, observe university rules and ethics, and complete studies within the approved duration.

Scholarship Oversight

The ASAC approves the number of staff pursuing further studies, develops scholarship administration guidelines, and oversees implementation of scholarship policies. The Senate provides final institutional oversight regarding academic and staff development matters.
