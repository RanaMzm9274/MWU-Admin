Admission Guide

Admission at Madda Walabu University

Admission is governed by the Madda Walabu University Senate Legislation 2024 and administered by the Senate, Registrar, Academic Programs Directorate, and Admissions and Placement Committee. Applicants are assessed through document verification, entrance eligibility, program capacity, and department-specific criteria.
