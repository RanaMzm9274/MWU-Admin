MA in TEFL

This program is part of Madda Walabu University's academic portfolio aligned with quality education, employability, research, and societal problem-solving.

Program Overview

MA in TEFL is offered by Madda Walabu University as part of its academic mission in teaching, research, community service, and professional preparation.

Duration and Course Structure

Postgraduate study normally combines core courses, specialization courses, seminars, research methods, and a thesis, project, practicum, or rotation component according to the curriculum. The curriculum document provides the detailed course list, credit structure, semester flow, and department requirements.

Thesis, Project, or Dissertation

Students complete all course requirements, maintain the required academic standing, finish the approved thesis, project, practicum, or rotation work, and complete final examination or defense requirements.

Admission and Student Readiness

Applicants are expected to meet MWU graduate admission rules, hold an appropriate first degree or equivalent preparation, and satisfy department screening, entrance, or professional requirements where applicable. Students should review the curriculum PDF for prerequisite background, semester sequencing, and department-specific rules before applying or registering.

Learning Outcomes

  • Build strong competence in critical reading, research, communication, civic responsibility, cultural understanding, legal and social analysis, and community engagement.
  • Apply classroom knowledge through assignments, laboratory, practicum, studio, field, clinical, or research activities where required by the curriculum.
  • Use ethical reasoning, teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills in professional and community contexts.

Assessment and Academic Progress

Assessment includes coursework, seminars, assignments, practical work where relevant, thesis or project supervision, and final approval by the department and graduate program procedures. Students are expected to satisfy attendance, academic standing, and graduation clearance requirements set by the university.

Career Pathways and Further Study

Graduates may pursue roles in teaching, media, public service, cultural institutions, legal and civic organizations, NGOs, research projects, and community development work. The program also supports further study, research, professional certification, or specialization depending on the graduate's field and performance.
