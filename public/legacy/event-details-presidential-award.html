About the event

In the history of Madda Walabu University, the first-ever Special Presidential Award Trophy has been awarded to a graduating student who achieved the highest overall score among all graduating students.

This recognition marks a major institutional milestone and celebrates excellence, commitment, and outstanding academic performance at university level.

  • First Special Presidential Award Trophy in MWU history.
  • Granted for the highest score among the university's graduating class.
  • Highlights MWU's commitment to merit, excellence, and academic distinction.
  • Intended to inspire future graduates toward exceptional achievement.
